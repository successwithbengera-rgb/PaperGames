What Is This Deck?

Chaos Control is one of the most iconic archetypes in Yu-Gi-Oh! history. Built around the banish mechanic, it uses Light and Dark monsters as fuel to summon powerhouses like Black Luster Soldier – Envoy of the Beginning and Chaos Emperor Dragon. The result is a mid-range control strategy that's both resilient and aggressive.

Why I Built This Proxy

The real cards for a proper Chaos build can run hundreds of euros — so this was a clear candidate for a proxy project. My goal was to build a playable, tournament-legal feeling version that captures exactly what makes the deck fun without breaking the bank.

I also love this deck because it perfectly illustrates a core game design principle: resource engines that feel risky to fuel. Every banish is a cost. That tension is what makes the deck interesting.

How It Plays

The opening turns are about establishing hand advantage and getting the right mix of Light and Dark monsters in the GY. Once you have two each, you're slamming BLS for a multi-attack swing or Emperor Dragon for a board wipe. The deck punishes passive opponents ruthlessly.
